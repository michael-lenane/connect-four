Connect Four

Rules

Only one piece can be played at a time
Players alternate turns placing their pieces
The game ends when there is a four-in-a-row or no spots left to place a piece

How to Play

Grab your piece by clicking and holding it
Drag your piece to the top of the column you would like to place it, and release.
After you place your piece, it is your opponent's turn to place their piece
